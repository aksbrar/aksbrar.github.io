What you need to know about spa!

A spa is a location where mineral-rich water is used for medicinal baths. Spas have been popular for centuries, offering treatments known as balneotherapy. They remain especially common in Europe and Japan.

  • Origins of the term
  • Treatments
  • Recent trends
  • Types of treatments

Origins of the term

The word spa comes from the Belgian town of Spa, known since Roman times for healing springs.

Treatments

Spa treatments are non-medical therapies aimed at improving health and relaxation.

Recent trends

Modern spas focus on beauty, wellness, and indulgence. The spa industry is growing worldwide.

Types of treatments

  • Day spa
  • Destination spa
  • Spa town
  • Foot spa
